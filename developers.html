DIDIDO?

The inspiration and development journey behind DIDIDO?

About LaBapps

My name is Nathan LaBar and I am a Marine Veteran, College Student (Graduate in August 2026), and the developer of Didido?. I have enjoyed working with computers since I was about 7 years old, but did not start my programming journey until I was 19 on deployment. I enjoy creating apps and prototypes that I would use in my daily life. Besides programming and schoolwork, I played for the FAU men's rugby team. I recently started a physical media music collection consisting of CDs and intend to create my own streaming library.

Why Didido?

Didido? was an unplanned inspiration that occurred when my girlfriend was frustrated trying to remember when the last time she had a car wash or replaced her water filter was. I instantly thought of a cars dashboard and lights that turn on at set intervals / when something needs to be done. I instantly went about designing how I would want to implement Didido?. With the help of Cursor, I quickly created a prototype and she really enjoyed the app and it became a priority for me to actually publish it on the App Store. It was exciting and a great learning experience to build an app I would use from the ground up and see it in the App Store.
